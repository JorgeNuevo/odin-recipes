Guacamole Recipe

Description

This is a classic, creamy Mexican dip made primarily from mashed ripe avocados combined with lime juice, salt, and various fresh ingredients.

It is a versatile and flavorful dish, popular globally as a dip for tortilla chips or a zesty topping for tacos, burritos, and other meals.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • White Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic
  • Limes
  • Jalape√±o
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice three ripe avocados in half, remove the pit.
  2. Peel back the avocado skin from the avocado flesh and gently mash them in a large bowl with the back of a fork to your desired level of smooth or chunky guac.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients and stir together with a spoon.
  4. Serve with chips for the most easy and delicious guacamole!
